# Figures

Generated output figures from `notebooks/art_brand_study.ipynb`. All figures produced at 150 DPI.

## Figures produced by the notebook

1. **`auction_performance.png`** — Bar chart of auction performance (% above low estimate) for each artist, colored by collaboration timing pattern. Red dashed line indicates the 150% threshold criterion.

2. **`kaws_attention.png`** — KAWS Google Trends search interest 2015-2022, with event markers for the Dior collab (blue dashed line, June 2018) and auction record (red dashed line, April 2019).

3. **`scenario1_collab_precedes.png`** — 3x3 grid of all 7 Scenario 1 artists (collab precedes auction). Each panel shows Google Trends over time with collab date marker and calculated lift ratio.

4. **`scenario2_no_collab.png`** — 2x3 grid of all 6 Scenario 2 artists (no brand collaboration). Each panel shows Google Trends with auction event marker and lift ratio.

5. **`scenario3_market_precedes.png`** — Side-by-side plot of Koons and Richter showing both the prior auction record (red dashed line) and the subsequent brand collaboration (blue dashed line).

6. **`three_scenario_comparison.png`** — Summary bar chart comparing average attention lift across the three scenario groups. The main takeaway figure of the study.
